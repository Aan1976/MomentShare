What happens next

We carefully review each application to ensure alignment with the MomentShare philosophy: presence, curiosity, and respect for meaningful conversation.

1 — Review

Your application is read manually. We look at communication style and motivation, not just background.

2 — Selection

If selected, you will be invited to a short private conversation with our team.

3 — Onboarding

Approved companions join the Founding Circle and receive onboarding details.

Our philosophy

MomentShare is not a gig platform. It is a curated circle of people who value meaningful human connection in Luxembourg. Selection is intentionally limited to maintain quality and trust.

You will hear from us within a few days. No need to follow up — every application is reviewed.
