The MomentShare Charter

A shared commitment to meaningful conversations, clear boundaries, and human respect.

Our purpose

MomentShare exists to create genuine human connection in a world where many people feel increasingly disconnected. We provide a space for thoughtful conversation between adults who value curiosity, respect, and presence. MomentShare is not dating. MomentShare is not therapy. MomentShare is not professional networking. It is simply meaningful time shared between two people.

The principles

01

Strictly platonic

Every MomentShare conversation is based on friendship, conversation, and mutual respect. Romantic expectations, flirting, or intimate requests are outside the purpose of the platform.

02

Respect above all

Every person deserves to feel comfortable, valued, and listened to. Disrespectful behaviour, pressure, or inappropriate comments are not accepted.

03

Privacy matters

Conversations are private. We do not record, analyse, publish, or share personal discussions.

04

Freedom to stop

Either participant may end a conversation at any moment if they no longer feel comfortable. No explanation is required.

05

Public first meetings

For in-person conversations, we encourage calm public locations such as cafés, cultural spaces, and community areas.

06

Authentic presence

Companions are not performers. They bring their own experiences, opinions, and personality into every conversation.

For companions

You are protected

You choose your availability, preferred locations, and conversation style. You are never required to continue a session that feels inappropriate.

You set boundaries

Clear boundaries create better conversations. Professionalism and kindness are expected from everyone.

You are valued

Companions are selected because their perspective and presence matter.

For guests

Come with curiosity

The best conversations happen when both people bring openness and respect.

Respect the person

A companion is not a service object. They are a person sharing their time and perspective.

Keep expectations clear

MomentShare provides companionship and conversation only.
